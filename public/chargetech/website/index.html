ChargeTech

ChargeTech is a new business dedicated to innovating and improving charging technology. Our goal is to make devices charge faster all the while saving and reusing energy, starting with our product ConstaCharge.

ConstaCharge

Have you ever ran out of battery power on your portable device when you really needed it? Maybe you were in the middle of an important online business meeting, or on a phone call with your friends. Whatever the case is, if you have experienced running out of battery, this product is for you.

Constantly charges your device while it's on, not only saving battery life but energy and money as well Valid for all mobile devices, whether that be a laptop, smartphone, or ipad. Never run out of battery life again and always be charging.
How it Works

ConstaCharge works by recycling the energy produced by your device into charging power, so as long as you are using your device, it will constantly be charging in the background.
